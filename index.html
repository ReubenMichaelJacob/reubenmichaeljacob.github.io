About Me

I'm a third-year Mechanical Engineering student at the University of Toronto with a strong interest in vehicle systems and mechatronics. I'm passionate about combining mechanical design with programming to develop smarter, more efficient transportation technologies. I have hands-on experience using SolidWorks for 3D modeling and prototyping, and I'm proficient in Python. I'm currently expanding my skills in C++, ROS2, and Node-RED to better integrate mechanical systems with intelligent control. I'm excited to keep building on these skills and make a meaningful impact through innovative engineering.

Recent Work

Modified RC Car
